tone

/tōn/

noun

1. a sound with reference to its pitch, quality, and strength.
2. the general character or attitude of a person or thing.

verb

1. give greater strength or firmness to.
2. harmonize with something in terms of color or sound.

noun

2. the point from which an activity or process is directed, or on which it is focused

About

Tone Center is a space based in sincere healing through thoughtful study and creative practice. We are dedicated to bringing together the community for a wide range of authentic healing arts classes taught by caring and focussed instructors who are not bound by any set of rules, or constrained by Tone Center in any way which might detract from the full benefit of each instructor's true skill and capability as a healer. Instructors decide their own curriculum. Tone Center was founded in 2018 by Samer Ghadry. Workshop Residency Opportunity Tone Center offers a unique opportunity for any qualified person to lead their own workshop. Interested teachers are invited to apply through email, Outlining and describing what you want to teach and what benefits you are looking to bring to the community. This is an opportunity for those creative and powerful teachers to have a place to share their own unique skills and outlooks with others.

People

Samer Ghadry is a musician, sound practitioner, and founder of Tone Center. Samer has spent the past 20 years studying and playing musics of wide variety. He has toured the world with Matthew Dear as percussionist and vocalist, and currently plays with long-time friend and collaborator Dave Harrington as a drummer. Samer graduated from the Sage Academy of Sound Healing, lead by life-long sound healer and teacher Lea Garnier. He has studied with John Beaulieu, Katie Down, Jessica Caplan, Michael Jay, and Don Conreaux.


Anastasia Frenkel is a physical re-patterner, energy healer, and student of the Daoist healing arts.

Recording

Tonecenter is also a recording studio, ready to help you create your own recordings whether they are for sampling use or a full album. We are perfect for smaller solo acoustic projects, and cannot accommodate full bands or very loud amplified playing. We bring expertise in overdubbing and creative solutions for recording almost any kind of music. Samer has been creating, playing, and recording music professionally for over 12 years with Matthew Dear, Dave Harrington, Yuka Honda and many more... listen to Samer's music
